## Vision

### 10x Check
The 10x version of Bharat MCP isn't just a data retrieval layer — it's India's business intelligence API for AI. Instead of "verify this GSTIN," it answers "should I do business with this company?" by synthesising signals across GST compliance, MCA filings, director networks, and filing recency into an actionable risk score with plain-language recommendations in Hindi and English.
